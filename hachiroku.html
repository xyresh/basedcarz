The Toyota corolla AE86




This car is a personal favourite after seeing it in Ireland,

where it is known as the "Twincam", this is an 80's Sports Car

equiped with the mighty 4A-GE engine. This engine's design was inspired

by the Famous Cosworth BDD engine, known for having belt driven cams and

an oversquared Piston Design, it was able to Rev quite high considering

it was based on a old A series block and with some yamaha trickery this 1.6L

engine produced 130hp and that is with a very understressed configuration

these engines later on where seen producing up to 240+ hp and revving up to 11k RPM!!!

and all of this in a N/A configuration!!


The chassis itself was quite simple and it didn't even have independent rear suspension

a characteristic which allowed this car to be popular in the world of drifting, that and it's RWD drivetrain


it was also quite popular thanks to the anime/manga series InitialD

and it spawned an entire generation of car enthusiasts.



So what makes this car BASED??


>it's Lightweight, with a curb weight of 1800lbs or 900kgs approximately
>it is as simple of a car as it gets
>you can drift it on any touge easily and enjoy the whole experience
>has a speed chime, jk had to add it in lol

>has one of the best 4 cylinder engines and sounds brilliant
>if you can find a descent example is quite reasonable to run and maintain despite it's almost 40 yr age!!
