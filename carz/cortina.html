The Ford Lotus Cortina Mk1




The Ford Cortina has a very Similar story to that of the Escort.It was an economy car built in Ford's UK factories Designed to be manufactured quickly and cheaply but it needed more marketing if it were to succeed. So the answer was a collaboration between Ford and british Sports and racecar manufacturer Lotus.The result was the lotus cortina. with some Work on the engine being done by Cosworth engineer Keith Duckworth making th engine a twincam 1300cc that produced about 105 hp with major chassis changes being made to it by Lotus and sharing many parts with the Lotus Elan this car was a rocketship(for the time). being the most succesfull BTCC car in that Era and winning many Rallies. This car was essentially the beginning of the golden Era of ford which lasted up till the late 90's of cheap Performance Cars For the people.
