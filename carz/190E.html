The lovechild between Daimler and Cosworth was the 190 Evo I and later the Evo II Bred to race DTM and to demolish the famous BMW M3 E30 of the era, the Evo II is now seen as one of the most successful racecars of all time. No one thought that an entry level Mercedes could be competitive around the track but oh boy it was. It really was the same story as we see today in F1 (won 16/24 races of the season) Luxury was not in the minds of the lead enginers. While yes the car had an optional 4-speed auto, no one bought it.
