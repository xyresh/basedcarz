The Royal Enfield bullet




The royal enfield bullet, the single longest produced motorcycle on earth!!! The Bullet was designed in 1931 in the uk and it's design was so good it continued being produced until even today!!!Nowadays it isn't anything special but back in the day it was a big deal Originally they were built in the uk but duriing the independence movement of india Production was moved to india in order for more of them to be built to patrol the india pakistan border and generally to patrol the country. Nowadays they are quite archaic with the single cylinder SOHC carbeurated 350cc engine. Despite it's mechanical shortcomings it is quite frankly a great and reliable machine to run. the design has stood the test of time and still stands stronger than anything else made. the only other motorcycle that could compete is the cub, but that design has changed alot more than the bullet over time.
