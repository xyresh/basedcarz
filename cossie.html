The Ford Escort Cosworth Mk1




The Ford Escort was ford's most popular European Car built in Essex

The regular Escort was a run of the mill grocery getter.

But soon it was realised that this car had a lot of potential with Ford's partnership

with the race engine manufacturer Cosworth.Cosworth originally Built the DFV for Formula 1 racing for the lotus team.

But the potential was realized very quickly to get a team of cosworth engineers to come up with a performance engine for the Escort.

The answer Was the famous 1.6L BDA engine. A 4 Cylinder NA Twincam engine. for the late 60's A twincam engine was only ever seen in Racing!

The engine Revved up to 9000RPM and produced a crazy 120+ HP thanks to a bore of 81mm and stroke of 77mm making it an oversquared design!

Fed air through 2 double barrell Weber Carbs essentially making it a ITB engine and having Belt driven Cams made it really unique and interesting

The combination of great chassis design by ford and this legendary engine gave this car multiple Rally wins Most notably the 1970 London to Mexico Rally.

It is quite Clear that this car was Legendary in it's time and enthusiasts of british Cars have this car on a pedestal.
