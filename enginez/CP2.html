The Yamaha CP-2 engine




The CP-2 engine, a recently introduced engine platform by yamaha(2014 with the MT-07). This engine can be seen everywhere nowadays in yamaha's Motorcycle Lineup and for very good reason. It is a 700cc, liquid cooled, DOHC, 2 Cylinder with a 270° crank order, which has the benefit of providing The same amount of torque as a V-twin equivalent, while keeping the design simple and compact.
The engine infact was so good, yamaha used it in multiple other motorcycles appart from the MT-07
The list is as follows

>Yamaha MT-07
>Yamaha XSR-700
>Yamaha R7
>Yamaha Ténéré 700
>Yamaha Tracer 7/7GT
And the list goes on!!!
